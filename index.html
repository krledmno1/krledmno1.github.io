As of December 2016 I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Information Security Group led by prof. David Basin at the ETH Zürich. Before that I was a PhD candidate in DeepSE research group at Politecnico di Milano, supervised by prof. Carlo Ghezzi. I have obtained my BSc degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science at Faculty of Electronic Engineering Niš and my MSc degree in Engineering Computing Systems at Politecnico di Milano.
My main research interests include developing techniques and methodologies that enable specification and verification of complex software systems.

Students interested in these fields are welcome to send me an email.
